More about me

I'm Michele (but also known as Mike): a Ph.D. student in the first National Italian PhD program in Artificial Intelligence at Campus Bio‑Medico Univesity and the University of Rome Tor Vergata. I'm also a visiting researcher at The University of Edinburgh.
In recent years, I've been a research fellow in “automatic image captioning for visual media contents” and a postgraduate researcher in “interpretability of neural network systems”.
I have a Master’s and Bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science.
